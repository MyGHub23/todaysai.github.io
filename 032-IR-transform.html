Industrial Revolution Transformations

The term "Industrial Revolution" refers to a series of profound technological and industrial changes that have significantly transformed economies and societies. As of last update in 2023, there have been four distinct industrial revolutions:       • First Industrial Revolution (late 18th to early 19th centuries)       • Second Industrial Revolution (late 19th to early 20th centuries)       • Third Industrial Revolution (late 20th century)       • Fourth Industrial Revolution (21st century)
