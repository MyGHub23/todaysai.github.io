An Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that deals with creating smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. Imagine a robot or a computer program that can think, learn, and make decisions like a human. That's what AI is about. To build AI, scientists use special computer instructions called algorithms. These algorithms guide the AI to learn from examples, much like how we learn from our experiences. For instance, AI can learn to recognize photos of animals by seeing lots of animal pictures. This learning ability is a key part of what makes AI special.

AI is not just about robots. It's used in many areas of our lives. For example, in healthcare, AI helps doctors diagnose diseases more accurately. In finance, it helps detect fraud. Even in our homes, smart devices use AI to make our lives easier.
