The Functioning of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence works like a smart assistant. It uses computers to do tasks that usually need human brains. These tasks can be things like solving problems or making decisions. AI uses special programs to do this, which are called algorithms. These algorithms enable AI learn from data. It's similar to how you learn to recognize a cat by observing many pictures of cats. The more pictures you see, the better you become at distinguishing whether you're looking at a cat or not. AI operates in the same way with data: it learns to identify patterns.

AI also uses something called machine learning. This means AI can get better over time. It learns from experience, just like humans. When AI gets new data, it can improve its algorithms to make better decisions in the future. Lastly, AI can understand and talk in human language. This part of AI is called natural language processing. It lets us talk to machines like we talk to people. This is how virtual helpers on phones can answer questions and follow commands.
