Editorial

In a time where Artificial Intelligence greatly influences our everyday lives, this book is an indispensable guide to understanding the wide-ranging aspects of Artificial Intelligence. It creates a firm foundation by breaking down AI's concepts and principles into detailed comprehensible parts. In this book, the author has skillfully illustrated and conveyed the concepts of AI in a simple and clear manner, ensuring that readers gain valuable knowledge. As such, it becomes a vital resource for anyone looking to attain a foundational yet in-depth knowledge of Artificial Intelligence.
